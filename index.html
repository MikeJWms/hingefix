The problem...

We love our M50 headphones, but they have one major design flaw - the hinges. Over time, the little tab in the hinge that keeps the ear cups from over rotating breaks. This prohibits the ear cup from cupping your ear. This effects all versions of the M50, original ATH-M50 headphones with the attached cable, ATH-M50x with the interchangeable cable, and the new ATH-M50xBT Bluetooth headphones.

That's why we built the hinge fix kit: A no-compromises solution that will keep our M50s on our ears for years.

The (Best) Solution

A cleverly designed bracket that replaces the hinge's broken tab

  • Doesn’t limit performance - ear cups can still fold in just like new
